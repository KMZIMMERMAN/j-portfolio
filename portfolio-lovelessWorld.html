the loveless world


This project was completed as part of a group project for J457. Our podcast, "the loveless world" explores the different types of love in our day-to-day lives and was inspired by bell hook's book, "all about love." We share one-of-a-kind stories from everyday people in your community whose lives have been touched—or forever changed—by deliberate acts of love.

All group members include: Katherine Zimmerman, Jonas Tijerino, Josh Standal, and Clayton Gerrans.

For the group project, I interviewed Courtney Marshalek, the Hartland Lakeside School District's outdoor education specialist, about her work with students and her love of the outdoors. Each group member was assigned to create a podcast episode featuring an interview with a key source. We each individually recorded and edited our own episodes to create a cohesive podcast.

My additional contributions to the group project were creating all branding and marketing materials for the podcast, including all graphics, the Instagram account, and the podcast's official website.
